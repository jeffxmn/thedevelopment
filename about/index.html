About the site

The Development is a website and blog dedicated to sharing stories about people and their websites, in interview form as inspired by The Setup.

Interviews

New interviews will be posted once a week on Mondays.

We regularly reach out to creators of websites that we find while browsing the Internet. We find websites through blogs, communities, and social networks. If you have any questions about, or suggestions for, an interview, reach out to us and we'll be glad to talk!

How can we keep up with The Development updates?

The best way is to follow us on Twitter, where we tweet everything that's happening.

You can also find an archive of all of our website-related announcements in our announcements page.

So what did YOU use to build this website?

Well, curious creature, this site is actually open sourced on GitHub, where it's also hosted, so feel free to take a look!

It's built using Jekyll, heavily modified with CSS.
